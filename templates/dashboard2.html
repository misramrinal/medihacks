About

Who we are

Our objective is to provide an inovative solution that collects health vitals and generates a fitness score using non-invasive methods or facial reading techniques.

  • Our solution is accurate, reliable, scalable, and can be deployed in various settings, from individual homes to large organizations
  • The wearable is non-intrusive, compact, and efficient methods have been found to make it cost effective
  • The circuit design is made with proper planning and a PCB is designed with the smd technology to ensure that it is compact and safe to wear

An SOS feature can be added which will send location of the watch to the family members in case of an emergency or an accident. The watch will be featured with different wireless protocols such as ANT+, and IEEE 802.15.4 for efficient data transfer. A small GSM module can also be added for calling and recieving calls as well as for 4G internet connections

First of all we built a model for predicting fitness using data from diverse source.

  • Data was processed, and exploratory data analysis was carried out..
  • It was discovered that health measures such as blood pressure, heart rate, and stress level have strong correlations with fitness level.
  • Even data acquired from a user's input via an app, such as height, weight, occupation, and gender, had an impact on the person's health.

  • All of the data collected from sensors was sent to MySQL database using PHP from ESP-32. via Wifi
  • This way the data would be secure and scalable and only available to those who require it.

Out of several algorithms Random Forest, Decision Tree, XGB Classifier,we found Multilayer Perceptron(MLP), a type of Artificial Neural Network gave best score of 93%, i.e out of 374 data samples it could correctly predict fitness level of about 349 data points in levels of 0(not fit), 1(need to achieve more),2(perfectly fit).

The model was deployed on website and an app named “Smart Wear” with an intuitive user interface where user can get all details of his/her health statistics and get fitness score on daily basis. Based on health stats, it would also instruct user how to achieve fitness goals.

We used VGG16 ,a deep learning model for stress detection using person emotions such as anger, happiness, disgust, neutral face which gave an accuracy of about 84%. We also used YOLO v5 for fast, accurate, and easy to use, for human face detection without any noise.

Why we used deep learning for stress detection?

  • Non-invasive: It will not interfere with safety of human body.
  • Feasible: deep learning-based approaches more feasible and cost-effective compared to sensors, which may require dedicated hardware and setup.
  • Real-time analysis: Deep learning-based face detection techniques can provide real-time analysis, enabling continuous monitoring of stress levels.
  • Facial expression analysis: Sensors, on the other hand, may not capture such nuanced facial expressions accurately.
  • Adaptability and generalization: This technique is capable of learning and adapting to different individuals and environments. In contrast, sensors may need customization or calibration in varying scenarios.

Services

What we do offer

Heart rate monitoring

The heartrate data is collected from MAX30100 sensor after proper callibration.The heartrate data is in BPM(Beats per Minute)

Daily step count

We used MPU6050 sensor and then determined the walking speed from acceleration and found out the average number of steps.

Body temperature measurement

For getting proper values of body temperature we used NTC thermistor of 10K resistance which works really well for calculating body temperature and offers a 95-98 percent accuracy

Blood Pressure systole and Diastole measurement

It is calculated from MAX32664 sensor which gives systole and Diastole pressure and our Blood pressure

Emotion detection through facial expression

We have implimented a Deep Learning model which can accurately determine the users Emotion through facial recognition

Determining stress level

All the above mentioned data that we get from various sensors are used simultaniously as input for our machine learning model which in turn determines the stress level of a person

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much accurate is our fitness score?

    Acording to the tests we ran, our model showed a 94% accuracy in the fitness score.

  • How often should I wear the device to get the most accurate fitness score?

    For the most accurate fitness score and comprehensive data analysis, it is advisable to wear the device consistently through out the day, especially during physical activites and sleep.Regular usage helps in collecting more data points and providing a better assessment of your health and fitness pattern.

  • What features does the device have apart from calculating fitness scores?

    Apart from calculating fitness score, our product also features heart rate monitoring, daily step count, blood pressure monitoring and body temperature determination. As our future score we also plan on adding GPS tracking, recieving notifications for calls and messages.

  • Is the device waterproof or water-resistant?

    No, currently its not waterproof, however we plan on adding such features in our future scope.

  • Are there any limitations or factors that may affect the accuracy of the fitness score?

    Yes, several factors can impact the accuracy of the fitness score, including the quality of sensors, proper device placement on the body, device calibration,and individual response in physiological responses. External factors such as environmental conditions, stress, and hydration levels can also influence the data collected.
